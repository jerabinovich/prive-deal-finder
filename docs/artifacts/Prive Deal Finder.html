🧮 JV Score Calculation Logic

Score = Urgency + Density + Owner + Location

Each deal receives a score from 0-100 based on weighted factors that predict JV partnership viability.

40% Regulatory Urgency

  • Unsafe structure posting: +20 points
  • Recert overdue >12 months: +15 points
  • Recert overdue 0-12 months: +10 points
  • 3+ violations: +10 points
  • 1-2 violations: +5 points
  • Fines >$50K: +5 points

30% Densification Gap

  • FAR utilization <50%: +30 points
  • FAR utilization 50-70%: +20 points
  • FAR utilization 70-90%: +10 points
  • Unit upside >100%: +5 bonus

FAR = Floor Area Ratio (actual built area ÷ allowable built area)

20% Owner Profile

  • Individual owner: +15 points
  • Small LLC (1-3 properties): +12 points
  • Family trust/estate: +10 points
  • Out-of-state owner: +5 points
  • Held >20 years: +3 bonus

10% Location Multipliers

  • Opportunity Zone: +10 points
  • Waterfront parcel: +5 points
  • Transit proximity (<0.5mi): +3 points
  • High-growth corridor: +2 points

💡 Interpretation Guide

  • 90-100: Immediate outreach - high pain, strong fit
  • 75-89: Strong prospect - monitor and contact
  • 60-74: Moderate opportunity - secondary priority
  • <60: Low priority - revisit if conditions change
